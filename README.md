## I build where product meets infrastructure

I'm a full stack engineer with a taste for clean interfaces, strong backend foundations, and AI that earns its place in the workflow.

My work usually lives at the intersection of:

- Product engineering that feels simple on the surface and solid underneath
- Full stack systems with thoughtful APIs, fast interfaces, and reliable data flows
- AI-assisted workflows that reduce friction instead of adding novelty
- Tools for teams, clinics, hiring, operations, and everyday work in growing markets

I care about shipping software that is polished enough to trust, practical enough to adopt, and flexible enough to evolve.
